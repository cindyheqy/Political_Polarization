PRAYER Reverend Erin Keys, Capitol Hill Presbyterian Church, Washington, D.C., offered the following prayer: Loving God, this holiday season, we pray that the abundance of Your bless-ings would be known by all, both our neighbors near and far. We pray for peace. We pray for joy. We pray for hope. We pray that the spirit of gen-erosity, forgiveness, and kindness that fills us this time of year might remain with us always, and that we would ex-tend to all people the grace we would hope to receive. As we look ahead to a new year that waits on the horizon, God, may we greet it with enthusiasm and a sense of purpose for Your work in our world. Amen.
